SkyDrago Airline Reservation System

Proposed System

The Proposed system ensures the complete freedom for users, where user at his own system can logon to this website and can book his ticket. Our proposed system allows only registered users to book the tickets, view timings and cancel their tickets.

Purpose

  • To automate the Flight ticket registration process without any physical human interaction.
  • To make the Flight ticket registration process accessible anywhere to the Users
  • To allow the admin to Cancel the ticket and efficiently manage their database in an effective manner.
